Dad's Garage
Project Using Node.js and Angular5

-Capstone Project at Claim Academy

This Capstone Project took a real world problem I had maintaining the four cars in Dad’s Garage since I change the oil on all the cars and do basic maintenance checks to keep the cars safe for our family members.

Dad’s Garage sends email alerts and creates calendar events for the seven (7) key factors to watch for each car - saving time and providing dad with peace-of-mind. The web app calculates and estimates when oil changes, tire rotations and 5 other reminders are provided so you don’t have to manually.



Dad’s Garage calculates mileage since the last oil change, tire rotation, oil check and tire check and automatically notifies the mechanic in the family to take action. It also calculates time since your last battery purchase, license plate tags were renewed and insurance card expiration date to again, keep the mechanic sleeping well at night.



The Garage Summary page gives you a visual of the cars in your garage with hover-over information that pops up on both Red and Green maintenance items - providing mileage or dates since last event check - thus giving you a head start on future alerts. This feature is especially useful when an oil change can be done ahead of schedule and before your daughter drives her car back to college - in another state!
